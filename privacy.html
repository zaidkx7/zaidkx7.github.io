CU-BlitZ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

What We Do

CU-BlitZ is a Chrome extension designed to enhance your university experience by automating evaluation forms and providing quick access to result pages. Our tool aims to streamline these processes while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

Permissions

CU-BlitZ requires the following permissions to function:

  • Scripting permissions to automate form interactions
  • Access to form elements for automated form filling
  • Tab and host access to navigate between university pages

Data Usage

We take your privacy seriously. CU-BlitZ does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All operations are performed locally within your browser, and no information is transmitted to external servers.
